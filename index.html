Our Products and Services

Commercial Water Treatment

We design sustainable water management plans, offering a full line of reverse osmosis and other treatment systems.

Solar Powering Systems

Installation of high-efficiency solar water pumps, coupled with a controller, and photovoltaic solar back-up systems.

Borehole Equipping Services

Supply and installation of equipment for borehole drilling, including control panels, submersible pumps, and underground cables.

Domestic Water Treatment

Full line of water treatment systems, including basic filtration, softeners, RO, and UV disinfection for domestic use.

Maintenance & Technical Support

Complete maintenance/technical support for your borehole, water, power, or solar systems to improve production efficiency.
